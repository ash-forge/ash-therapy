# Public Mental Health Research Sources

**Curated list of legitimate, publicly accessible mental health research and clinical materials for ash-therapy training data.**

---

## Government & Public Health Organizations

### United States

**NIMH (National Institute of Mental Health)**
- Website: https://www.nimh.nih.gov/
- Resources: Research papers, clinical trials data, educational materials
- License: Public domain (U.S. government work)
- Content: Depression, anxiety, PTSD, bipolar, schizophrenia research

**SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration)**
- Website: https://www.samhsa.gov/
- Resources: Treatment Improvement Protocols (TIPs), clinical guides
- License: Public domain (U.S. government work)
- Content: Evidence-based treatment protocols, crisis intervention

**CDC Mental Health Resources**
- Website: https://www.cdc.gov/mental-health/
- Resources: Public health research, prevention strategies
- License: Public domain (U.S. government work)
- Content: Mental health surveillance, community interventions

**NIH PubMed Central (PMC)**
- Website: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/
- Resources: Open access peer-reviewed research papers
- License: Open access (various licenses)
- Content: Clinical psychology, psychiatry, therapy outcomes
- Filter: "Free full text" option for open access papers

**Veterans Affairs PTSD Research**
- Website: https://www.ptsd.va.gov/
- Resources: PTSD treatment guides, research summaries
- License: Public domain (U.S. government work)
- Content: Trauma therapy, evidence-based PTSD treatments

## Academic Open Access Repositories

**PubMed Central Open Access Subset**
- URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/tools/openftlist/
- Content: ~7 million open access biomedical articles
- Search: "cognitive behavioral therapy" OR "depression treatment" OR "anxiety intervention"
- License: CC-BY, CC0, public domain (check per article)

**PLOS ONE (Mental Health Section)**
- Website: https://journals.plos.org/plosone/
- License: CC-BY (open access)
- Content: Peer-reviewed psychology and psychiatry research
- Search: Mental health, therapy outcomes, clinical interventions

**Frontiers in Psychology**
- Website: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology
- License: CC-BY (open access)
- Content: Clinical psychology, psychotherapy research
- Note: All articles open access after publication

**BMC Psychiatry**
- Website: https://bmcpsychiatry.biomedcentral.com/

- Content: Clinical psychiatry, therapy research

**JMIR Mental Health**
- Website: https://mental.jmir.org/

## Quality Criteria

**Every source must meet these requirements:**
- ✅ Publicly accessible (open access or public domain)
- ✅ Peer-reviewed OR from recognized mental health organization
- ✅ Evidence-based (not opinion or anecdotal)
- ✅ No patient medical records or private data
- ✅ Clear licensing (can legally use for training)

**Exclusion criteria:**
- ❌ Copyrighted without permission
- ❌ Patient medical records
- ❌ Non-peer-reviewed blogs or forums
- ❌ Synthetic or AI-generated content
- ❌ Unverified online sources

## Licensing Notes

**Public Domain:**
- U.S. government publications (NIMH, SAMHSA, VA, CDC)
- Works published before 1928
- Authors who explicitly released to public domain

**Open Access (CC-BY, CC0):**
- PLOS journals
- Frontiers journals
- BMC journals
- Many PubMed Central articles
- Can use with attribution

**Requires Permission:**
- Copyrighted therapy manuals (Beck Institute, Linehan's DBT)
- Professional training programs (ASIST, QPR)
- Commercial publications
- Action: Contact for licensing or use only freely available excerpts
